SoHey – Child Safety Standards

At SoHey, we are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users and have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Prohibited Content and Behaviour
   SoHey strictly prohibits any content or activity that involves:

* Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
* Exploitation or endangerment of minors
* Any attempt to engage minors in inappropriate or illegal activity

Any such content or behaviour will result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to relevant authorities.

2. User Reporting
   Users can report inappropriate behaviour or safety concerns directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly, and appropriate action is taken.

3. Moderation and Enforcement
   We actively monitor and review user reports and take enforcement action including:

* Account suspension or termination
* Removal of content
* Reporting to law enforcement where required

4. Compliance with Laws
   SoHey complies with all applicable child safety and reporting laws in relevant jurisdictions.

5. Contact
   For any child safety concerns, please contact:
   [info@sohey.app](mailto:info@sohey.app)
